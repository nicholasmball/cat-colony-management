Behaviour, permissions & a11y

  • Reached from an admin ⚙️ Organisation nav item (admins only); non-admins are redirected away (RLS-backed, not just hidden).
  • Name required (non-empty); notes optional. Save shows a success banner; SubmitButton prevents double-submit.
  • Renaming updates the home card and anywhere the org name appears.
  • Labelled fields, ≥44px targets, responsive 375/768/1280.

Deferred / decision

  • Delete / deactivate org: proposed out of MVP — it cascades to all colonies, cats and feeding history and could lock the only admin out. Revisit with a guarded "archive" later.
  • Logo: waits on image storage (Photo/R2 task). Alert thresholds & urgency tiers: their own admin screens.
