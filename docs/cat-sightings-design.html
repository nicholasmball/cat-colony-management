Cat sightings — seen / not-seen / concern

Building on the Requirements. Part of the feeding update: for each cat the feeder can mark Seen, Not seen or Concern — each writes one append-only cat_sighting tied to the feeding event. This is the raw signal the missing-cat review and alerts build on. (Backfill — built & live, commit 9b5ef05.)

Behaviour, semantics & a11y

  • Toggle: tapping the selected state again clears it (no accidental forced choice).
  • Append-only cat_sightings — never updated/deleted from the client; offline-idempotent.
  • Not-seen does NOT change cat status — missing-cat status needs human review after repeated absences (separate task); sightings are just the evidence.
  • Concern is a soft flag for the caretaker to follow up — not an incident (incidents are their own flow).
  • State by colour and label (green/amber/red + text); ≥44px targets; works at 375px.
