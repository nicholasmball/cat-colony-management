4. The reusable pattern

One <EmptyState> building block — round icon, bold one-liner, a muted explanatory line, and an optional primary CTA. Variants differ only by icon, copy, and whether a CTA is passed (managers yes, feeders no). This keeps every future list (schedules, incidents…) consistent when those cards add their own.

  • Information scent: copy says what a colony/cat is and the button says exactly what happens ("Add your first colony", not "OK").
  • No disabled buttons: a role that can’t act sees explanatory text, never a greyed CTA (WCAG-friendly, per our constraints).
  • Accessibility: real <h2>/heading, CTA ≥44px, icon is decorative (aria-hidden), meaning carried by text not colour.
  • Mobile-first: centred block, comfortable tap target, works at 320px.

5. Acceptance-criteria coverage

  • New org (manager, 0 colonies) → welcome panel + single CTA to first available step → §1, §2.
  • Feeder with nothing → guidance, no create CTA → §1 third phone, §3 second phone.
  • Colonies empty + cats empty → designed states → §3.
  • Schedule step shown, not linked → §1 "soon" tag.
  • firstRunStep pure + tested → §1 note.
